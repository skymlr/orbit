# Menu Bar Override

## Intent

This surface is the launch pad. It should feel compact, high-confidence, and immediately actionable.

## Rules

- Width stays `360pt`.
- Outer shell uses the master `14pt` panel radius and dark glass at `0.44`.
- When there is no active session, the hero button dominates the surface.
- When a session exists, the layout shifts to a two-action split: `Session` primary, `Capture` secondary.
- Supporting metadata remains caption-sized and subdued.
- The surface should never become scroll-heavy or settings-dense.

## Avoid

- Do not add more than two primary actions.
- Do not use full-width tables, settings forms, or long explanatory copy here.
