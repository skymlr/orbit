# Session History Override

## Intent

History is read-only archive space. It should feel calmer and slightly less energized than the live session view.

## Rules

- History cards use `thinMaterial` more often than live editing surfaces.
- The current-session banner is the one place where live-state cyan emphasis is allowed inside history mode.
- Session strip items stay compact, horizontally scrollable, and `10pt` rounded.
- History task rows preserve task card hierarchy but should not show live editing affordances.
- Calendar cells use `6pt` radius with cyan reserved for selectable and selected states.

## Avoid

- Do not reuse live editing controls in archived lists.
- Do not turn history into a dense analytics dashboard.
